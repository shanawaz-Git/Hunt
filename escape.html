a new year wishMy G.U.N.D.U+

Hello Rag-V — my G.U.N.D.U: Gorgeous, Unique, Nice-hearted, Delightful, Unstoppable. At this exact moment, when the clock and the calendar lined up to 11:11, 1/1, I want to wish for both of us to be happy, smiling, laughing — together through all of it, the good and the bad.

Even on the days we hurt each other, I want us to keep choosing to fix it. My wish for this year is to hold your hand, take care of you, make you feel at home, make you laugh like no one else can, and be your shoulder whenever you need one.

I don't know what's planned for us, but I hope it's you.

I love you, buboooo

happy birthdayFor Rag-V, on her day+

I hope today reminds you of how special you truly are. You bring so much warmth, joy, and meaning into my life without even trying. Being around you feels easy, real, and comforting — like a place I want to stay.

I love your smile, your strength, and the way you make ordinary moments feel important. All I want today is for you to feel loved, valued, and genuinely happy.

No matter where life takes us, a part of my heart will always smile because of you.

Happy birthday, Rag-V 💗

what you told me"You are one big loosu"+

Hi, Shanu. You are one big loosu. Please don't be mad at me, I just wanted to tell you what you mean to me.

You're the first person who loved talking to me. The first person who valued me the way I'd always hoped to be valued. The first person who came out with me, who went to a café with me, who brought me flowers, who brought me gifts.

The first person who tolerated me and stayed. Who accepted me exactly as I am, and thought well of me anyway. The first person who showed me what real care feels like — the first I hugged, and the first to hug me back.

You never let me feel alone. There are still so many things left to say.

what i told you"We chose friendship"+

Hey Rag-V, I don't know how to start this, so I'll just say it the way I feel it.

Something shifted in my life that I didn't see coming. You happened. And nothing's been the same since — not the way I laugh, not the way I see myself, not the way I believe in people.

I know I haven't always been easy. There were moments I made things harder than they needed to be. I see that clearly now, and I carry it. But through all of it, you stayed.

We chose friendship, and I want you to know — I'm choosing it fully. Not as a consolation, not as a way to stay close just in case, but because losing you completely is something I refuse to let happen.

You changed me, Ragave. You gave me the ability to laugh from my stomach, to feel things I thought I couldn't feel, to believe I'm worth something to someone. So here I am — your loosu friend — telling you that you mean the entire world to me.

I love you, bubooo. Always will. In every version of us.

2:16 in the morningA dream I had+

I just woke up from the most beautiful dream. We were a family, living a happy, healthy life, full of laughter. I could hear a child's laughter through the whole dream.

I don't think anything else could make me feel that way except you. If there's ever a chance, and if it's meant to be, I want you to be my partner for the rest of my life.

I know there might be complications — with us, with our families — and I know that scares you. But deep down, I've already registered you as my soulmate, and nothing changes that from my end.

I'm so blessed to have you, and I ask every god I know to let me keep you, for good.

just gratitudeThank you for being my home+

Thank you for being my home. Thank you for being my best. Thank you for being my reason to smile. Thank you for being my reason to work.

Thank you for choosing me. Thank you for being with me.

No words can really describe what you're worth to me.
