The Year of Our Lord, 2026
Extraordinary People, Extraordinary News

Dearest Gentle Reader, it has often been said that while many stars attempt to shine, only a true Diamond truly dazzles. This author has recently come into possession of a series of confessions regarding a certain Rag-V—a Diamond who appears to have changed the very perspective of one admirer's soul.

It seems our subject is known by a most curious and affectionate title: G.U.N.D.U.—Gorgeous, Unique, Nice-hearted, Delightful, and Unstoppable. A title that, much like the wearer, carries a weight of perfection that few in Mayfair could hope to match.

This Author finds it most touching that such a bond has been cemented by a series of thoughtful tributes—tokens of affection ranging from Kashmir Jumkas and Attar to Crochet Bouquets and Real Flowers. Each gift, it seems, is a chapter in a story of a life fundamentally changed.

For the admirer in question, this Diamond is not merely a friend, but a "Home"—a safe harbor of understanding that belongs to her alone. There are those who speak of soulmates as if they are found only in dreams, but I am told of a vision where a happy, healthy life filled with laughter was so real one could almost hear it.

It is a sincere prayer to the heavens to hold a hand for life, to fix what is broken, and to care for a soul that truly deserves the world. As the season continues, we shall see if destiny aligns with these heartfelt resolutions. For now, let us celebrate a love that refuses to be ordinary.

Yours truly,
Lady Whistledown
