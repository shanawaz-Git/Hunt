A quiet place for the two of you

Your notes and photos live in your own Google Drive — nothing is stored anywhere else. Connect once and everything saves straight to a private folder only this app can touch.

One-time setup needed. Add your Google OAuth Client ID in the code (CLIENT_ID near the top of the script) and host this file on an authorised domain. Setup steps are in the message from Shanu's build notes.
Uses the drive.file scope — this app can only see files it creates for you, never the rest of your Drive.
